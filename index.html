01 — Problem

Sleep is broken. Dreams are invisible.

A third of adults don't get enough sleep (CDC), costing the global economy over $585 billion annually in lost productivity. Yet the solutions available today — apps like Calm or Sleep Cycle — only track when and how long you sleep. They ignore the other half of the equation: what happens in your mind while you do.

We forget 95% of our dreams within ten minutes of waking. That's a daily window into our emotional patterns, recurring anxieties, and subconscious life — completely lost. No app currently bridges the gap between sleep data and dream meaning.

02 — Solution

An AI that turns dreams into experiences.

Reverie is an AI-powered sleep companion that does what no competitor does: it combines smart sleep scheduling with an intelligent dream journal that makes your dreams come alive. Log a dream, and Reverie generates a visual storyboard, a custom soundscape, and a narrated bedtime story — all drawn from your own subconscious.

🌙
Smart Sleep Scheduling
Personalized sleep windows based on your chronotype, age, and calendar.
🎨
Dream → Visual Storyboard
AI-generated images from your dream description using DALL-E / Stable Diffusion.
🎵
Dream → Soundscape
ElevenLabs audio generation creates an ambient soundscape from your dream's mood.
📖
Dream → Bedtime Story
GPT-4 rewrites your dream as a calming narrated story to fall asleep to.
🔒
Focus Mode
App lock screen prevents distraction during your sleep window (full Android support).
🧠
Pattern Analysis
NLP identifies recurring themes, emotions, and locations across your dream journal.
